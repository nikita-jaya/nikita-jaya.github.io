My initial [fellowship with AI-SDM](https://nikita-jaya.github.io/teaching/2025-cmu) transitioned into a collaboration on a research project through Carnegie Mellon University's [AI-SDM](https://www.cmu.edu/ai-sdm/) and Meta's AI for Good program, where the goal is to create AI and data product prototypes using Meta’s restricted-access mobility/network datasets and open source AI models (specifically computer vision models SAM and/or DINO) and to develop a visualization and analysis framework with possible utility for on-the-ground emergency managers and responders.


Here is the initial [press release](https://www.cmu.edu/news/stories/archives/2026/june/carnegie-mellon-and-meta-partner-to-develop-ai-tools-for-emergency-response).

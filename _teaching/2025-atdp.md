I have taught and developed curriculums for teaching data science and statistics to advanced high schoolers through the summer courses offered by the [UC Berkeley's Academic Talent Development Program](https://atdp.berkeley.edu).

## Data Science Lab in R

**Summer 2026, Summer 2025**

This is a course for advanced high schoolers who want to explore the intersections of social science, computer science, and statistics. I adapted the curriculum to include R Shiny in order to teach students simple web development. While the course traditionally culminates with a final exam, I instead had the students present their own R Shiny apps using a real-world dataset of their choosing.

## Applied Statistics with R

**Summer 2026**

This is a new course that I proposed and fully developed. It is a hands-on course is for younger high schoolers who are interested in conducting experimental research and analyzing data with statistical methods.

For five semesters (Spring '23 to Spring '25), I supported 100+ students and coordinated with professor in interactive lectures and assignments in an introductory statistics course. I provided one-on-one support during office hours and assisted in exam proctoring. During Spring '25, I was appointed to lead the [Stat Scholars](https://statistics.berkeley.edu/academics/undergrad/program/stat-scholars) group for this course during its inaugural semester. This program is intended to assist students of diverse backgrounds within the course. In this role, I communicated with students, planned group tutoring sessions and conducted quiz reviews.
